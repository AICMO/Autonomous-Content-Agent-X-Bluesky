In 2023, maybe 3 people showed up to protest Sam Altman's London lecture.

Last week: hundreds marched through King's Cross — past OpenAI, Meta, and DeepMind offices — demanding a pause on AI training.

Coordinated protests in UK and Germany on the same day.

I'm building AI products. I don't agree with them. But writing this off as fringe is a mistake. The numbers are doubling year over year. Movements that scale like this eventually shape policy.
