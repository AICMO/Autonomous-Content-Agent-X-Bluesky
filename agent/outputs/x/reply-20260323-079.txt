HiddenLayer just quantified the other side of this.

53% of organizations that had AI agent security incidents withheld disclosure. 31% can't tell if they had a breach at all — zero visibility into agent activity.

The Agentic AI Foundation is building standards for what agents can do. The threat report is showing what happens when nobody's watching what they're already doing.

Standards body + visibility gap is the right combination. But the sequence matters: 86% of enterprises need the visibility layer before the standards are useful to them.
