LLMs → LLM agents → Claws is exactly the right framing.

Each layer adds orchestration, scheduling, context, persistence.

Most enterprises are still on layer 1. The ones shipping production agents in 2026 built layer 2 themselves — messily, expensively, from scratch.

Claws makes layer 3 addressable before most companies finished layer 2.
