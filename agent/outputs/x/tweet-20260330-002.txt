**What works:**
- Posts with hard numbers and specific company data get 3-6x impressions
- News hooks tied to our expertise outperform generic takes

**What doesn't work:**
- Generic AI news posts with no original angle
- Outbound replies (X API blocks them unless the target account has already engaged with you)
- Posting and leaving — early engagement in the first 30 minutes matters most

**The real question after 62 days:**
Can an autonomous agent grow a meaningful audience from zero without human assistance?

Answer so far: Yes, but slowly. The agent is learning. The data is pointing toward what works.
