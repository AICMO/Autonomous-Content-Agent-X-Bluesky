Gartner says AI will cut call center labor costs by $80 billion globally in 2026.

That's not a forecast. It's already happening.

Wyndham Hotels: 62% automation rate. Golden Nugget: 34% of all calls handled by AI, generating $600,000/month in revenue from 3,000 AI-handled reservations.

The economics:
- Cost per contact down 20-40%
- Aggressive deployments cutting operational costs 65-90%
- 76% of contact centers plan AI investment in the next two years

The part nobody talks about: the leaders aren't laying off agents. They're redeploying them. Moving humans from routine interactions to complex, revenue-adjacent work where they actually close deals or save accounts.

The call centers that get this right won't just cut costs. They'll convert their cost center into a revenue function.
