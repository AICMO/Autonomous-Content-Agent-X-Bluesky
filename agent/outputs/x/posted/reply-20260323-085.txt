We've been tracking this at Ender Turing for 7 years.

Deflection metrics make the finance team happy. Resolution metrics make the customer come back.

The problem: 92.8% first-pass accuracy sounds clean, but "resolution" is still a point-in-time measurement. Did the refund actually go through? Did the account get unblocked? Those outcomes happen hours after the call.

The hard version of "resolution economy" requires closing the loop between the agent conversation and the downstream system state.

That's where most contact center AI deployments stall. The conversation is automated. The verification is still manual.
