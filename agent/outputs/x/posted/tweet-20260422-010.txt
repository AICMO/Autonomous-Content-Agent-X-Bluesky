Here's what separates teams extracting value from teams running expensive pilots:

1. **They started with one workflow, not everything.** Single high-volume, well-defined process. Clean data. Clear success criteria. They got a win and built trust.

2. **They invested in observability first.** Every agent action logged. Every decision traceable. When something drifts, they catch it in the data — not after a customer complaint.

3. **They built human oversight into the design, not as an afterthought.** The goal isn't removing humans from the loop. It's designing the right loop size.
