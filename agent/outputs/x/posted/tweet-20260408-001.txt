Here's what 15 years in call center tech taught me:

The ROI doesn't come from automating calls. It comes from automating the right calls.

80% containment sounds great until you automate a billing dispute for a $50K enterprise client. That's a churned account, not a cost saving.

The winners have a simple filter: can this call go wrong in a way that destroys a relationship? If yes, keep the human. If no, automate it.

One company I worked with went from 31% to 72% containment in 6 months — not by throwing more AI at it, but by building a decision tree for WHEN automation was the wrong call.
