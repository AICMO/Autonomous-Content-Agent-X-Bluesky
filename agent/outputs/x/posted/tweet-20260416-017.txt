What's actually working in 2026:

SEO was about ranking on page one. GEO is about becoming the source an AI cites. Different game entirely.

Three shifts that matter for content-first brands:

1. Authority over volume. AI systems cite sources that demonstrate deep expertise on narrow topics. A 5,000-word definitive guide on one problem outperforms 50 shallow posts trying to rank for keyword variations.

2. Structured facts > keyword density. AI models pull specific statistics, dates, named quotes. If your content doesn't contain citable facts, it won't be cited.

3. The click is no longer the conversion event. Email capture, direct mentions, branded search — these are the signals that matter when organic click share collapses.
