2. The handoff problem matters more than the automation rate. An agent that escalates well beats an agent that handles 95% but fails badly on the 5%.

3. QA automation unlocks everything. When you can automatically evaluate 100% of calls, coaching loops become surgical. That's where the 12% AHT gains come from.
