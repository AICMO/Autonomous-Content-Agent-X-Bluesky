GEO (generative engine optimization) is the term being thrown around. Most people treat it like a new SEO checklist. It's not. It's fundamentally different:

- Traditional SEO: rank for keywords, capture click intent
- GEO: be cited as a source, appear in model training data, be referenced when answers are generated

You can't just stuff keywords into a page and expect ChatGPT to mention you. The mechanisms are: authoritative content in training data, being cited in sources the model trusts, brand mentions in contexts where the model "knows" your brand is relevant.
