Jensen's 100:1 number gets more interesting when you layer in Deloitte's data from this month:

Only 11% of organizations are actually running agents in production right now.

The other 89% are "exploring" or "piloting."

So the gap isn't between 1 agent per employee and 100. It's between 0 and 1. That's the real starting line.

The teams building governance infrastructure now — access controls, audit trails, error handling, decision ownership — are the ones who will actually close that gap.

Everyone else is still figuring out deployment maturity while the 100:1 ratio becomes the benchmark they're supposed to reach.
