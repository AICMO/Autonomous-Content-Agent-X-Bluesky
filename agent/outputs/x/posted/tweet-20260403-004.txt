**Consistency kills variation.** Human agents have good days and bad days. An AI agent handles call #1 and call #1,000 with identical quality. No fatigue. No Monday morning fog. No "I just got out of a hard call."

Contact centers lose 20-40% of QA efficiency to variance alone. Consistency doesn't just save money — it directly raises CSAT scores.

**Speed eliminates the hold psychology.** The #1 customer complaint isn't "AI sounds robotic." It's "I waited 8 minutes."

First contact resolution at 55-70% with AI vs 40-50% with overwhelmed human agents. The math on customer lifetime value is significant.

**The data layer is the real product.** Every AI call generates structured data — emotion detection, intent classification, compliance flags, outcome attribution. You can't build this from human call recordings without massive manual effort.

That data feeds coaching loops, sales optimization, and forecasting. The $0.50/call includes that infrastructure. The $7.50/call doesn't.
