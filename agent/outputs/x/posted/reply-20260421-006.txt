The call center version of this is striking.

A single AI conversation costs $0.03-0.50 end-to-end. Sounds like a solved problem. But at 50,000 calls per day, you're generating 50K context windows per day. The per-token price is 50x lower than 2022 — but you're running 200x more tokens per call than a simple chatbot query.

Net result: same monthly bill. Different architecture.

The teams that crack this aren't the ones with the best models. They're the ones that instrument every call to identify which interactions are token-heavy and which are token-light — then route accordingly.

Routing isn't glamorous. It's where the unit economics actually live.
