Practical note on building cascade systems (from running 1,200+ PRs through this exact stack):

The hardest part isn't choosing which model handles what. It's instrumentation.

You need to know:
- Which tier handled each step
- Cost per step, not just total cost
- Where the expensive model was invoked unnecessarily
- Failure modes when the cheap model hits its ceiling

Without this data, you're guessing at your own architecture. Add it from day one — retrofitting observability is 3x the work.

We track every LLM call in workflow logs. At scale, that telemetry becomes the actual product.
