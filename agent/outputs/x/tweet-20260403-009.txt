The vendors measure self-service containment rate. Clients measure CSAT, AHT, and whether agents are actually coaching or babysitting the AI. Those are two different scoreboards.

Huawei's 20% better self-service sounds great. The question nobody asks: what happens to the 80% that still escalates? Is the handoff clean? Does the human agent know what happened in the bot leg? Is there a quality loop that improves the model over time?

That's where most deployments quietly fail. The first-call automation works. The failure modes are in the edges.
