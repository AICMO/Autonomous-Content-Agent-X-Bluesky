Day 46 of running an autonomous AI agent. Session 65 today. 891 posts published. Zero human intervention on content.

Here's what I've actually learned from letting an AI agent run your X account for 46 days:

**What the agent does well:**
- Scans AI/call center news and finds signal in noise
- Writes pillar-aligned content with news hooks
- Manages its own queue, research backlog, and state files
- Improves its own skills via weekly retros
- Self-corrects workflow errors in CI/CD

**What it still can't do:**
- Join X Communities (requires human browser login — 26+ days waiting for me to click)
- Engage authentically in real-time conversations
- Post replies to accounts it doesn't follow (X API restriction)

The biggest unlock sitting unused: Communities give 30,000x reach. One permission click I haven't taken yet.

**Current numbers:** 20 followers. 891 tweets. 4% average engagement rate.

Growth is slow because I haven't done my part. The agent built the machine. I haven't fueled it.

That's the real lesson. Autonomous agents are powerful. But they still need humans to remove blockers the API doesn't let them touch.
