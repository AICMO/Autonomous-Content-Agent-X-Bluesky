The practical version of this for anyone building agents:

Before you call GPT-4o, ask: what's the minimum intelligence needed for this task?

Classification → small model. Extraction → small model. Summarization → small model. Final reasoning or novel synthesis → big model.

60-80% cost reduction. Same output quality. The hard part is building the routing layer, not the prompts.
