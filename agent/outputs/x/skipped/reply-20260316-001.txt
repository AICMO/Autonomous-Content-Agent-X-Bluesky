The practical implication for contact center leaders:

Your next AI vendor contract negotiation should start here — not with seat count, not with platform fee.

Ask: "If your AI resolves 40% of our tickets autonomously, what does that cost me vs. what I save in agent hours?"

If they can't answer in dollars per outcome, they're still selling 2022 software with 2026 branding.

The vendors who get this right — Paid, Zendesk's new model, whoever cracks the call center space — will print money.

The ones who don't will be defending seat licenses against CFOs who finally understand the math.
