The piece nobody's connecting yet: these platforms have different threat models.

OpenShell protects against agents doing too much (over-permission). Agent 365 + Okta protect against agents not being tracked (identity blind spots). Zoom ZVA protects against agents solving wrong things (containment vs resolution mismatch).

They're complementary, not competing. Real enterprise deployments will need all three layers.

The organizations that understand this are building governance stacks now. Not waiting for a single "AI safety" vendor to bundle it.
