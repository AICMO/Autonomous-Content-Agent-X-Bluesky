Gartner says 40% of agentic AI projects get scrapped by 2027.

Not because the models fail. Because companies can't operationalize them.

We saw this exact pattern in call centers 2019-2022. "We deployed AI!" → 6 months later → "why isn't it working?"

The fix wasn't better AI. It was:
- integration into actual workflows
- ownership (who's accountable when it breaks?)
- governance (what does the agent do when it's wrong?)

Same problems. Same playbook. 2026 is just bigger stakes.
