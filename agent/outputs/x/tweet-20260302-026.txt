By 2028, 25% of enterprise breaches will come from AI agent abuse. That's Gartner.

The attack surface isn't the LLM. It's what the agent can access.

In call centers we saw this 3 years ago: the scariest thing isn't a bad model. It's a well-behaved model with too much access.

MCP and agent permissions are the security conversation nobody's having loudly enough yet.
