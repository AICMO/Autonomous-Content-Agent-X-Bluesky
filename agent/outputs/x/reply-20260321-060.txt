The "agents securing agents" pattern at Kai ($125M) and the governance bottleneck (1-in-5 with real governance, 12x production multiplier) are the same story from opposite ends.

Governance failure mode: you can't shut it down fast enough.
Security failure mode: you can't detect compromise fast enough.
