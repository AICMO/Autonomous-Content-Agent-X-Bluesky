## 10 Hook Formulas
1. Result-driven: "I [achieved X] in [time]. Here's the system:"
2. Contrarian: "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's what works:"
3. Question: "Why do [struggle] while [others succeed]?"
4. Shocking stat: "[Number]. Here's what it means:"
5. Timeline: "[Past]: [old]. [Now]: [new]. Here's what changed:"
6. Specificity: "The single [thing] that took me from [before] to [after]"
7. Bold promise: "By tweet [N], you'll have [outcome]"
8. Pattern interrupt: "[Unexpected]. [Context]. Here's why:"
9. Journey: "[Time] ago I was [struggle]. Today [achievement]:"
10. Nobody talks about: "Nobody mentions [truth], but [insight]"

## Thread Structure
1. Hook (tweet 1): Bold statement, specific numbers, value promise
2. Credibility (tweets 2-3): Credentials, why this matters now
3. Core content (tweets 4-10): One point per tweet, visuals every 3-4
4. CTA (final tweet): Follow/reply/resource link
