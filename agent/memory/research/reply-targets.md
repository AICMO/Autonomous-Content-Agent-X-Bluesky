## Reply Writing Notes

**What works (confirmed from data):**
- Short, specific personal data points ("I shipped 650+ PRs with this pattern")
- Contrarian with evidence ("Everyone says X, but I measured Y")
- Questions that invite reply-back ("Did you see the same thing with [specific scenario]?")
- Call center AI expertise angle — unique, not many X accounts with 7yr call center AI background

**What to avoid:**
- Generic agreement ("Great point!")
- Self-promotion in reply ("Check out my repo")
- Replies to posts >24h old
- Replies when either queue >15

**Voice reminder:**
- Use contractions and fragments
- Start with "And" or "But" sometimes
- No em dashes
- No "Not just X, it's Y" construction
