## Hybrid Model Inevitability

### Why Pure AI Fails
- **Complex edge cases:** Require human judgment
- **Emotional escalation:** Angry customers need human empathy
- **High-value transactions:** Customers want human reassurance
- **Legal/compliance:** Some decisions require human accountability

### The Winning Formula
- **AI handles:** Routine inquiries, status checks, simple transactions (70-80% of volume)
- **Humans handle:** Escalations, complex problems, emotional situations (20-30% of volume)
- **Result:** 80% cost reduction + 90 NPS (best of both)

**Our approach:** Ender Turing designed for hybrid from day one

## Production Deployment Challenges

### Top Blockers (2026)
1. **Integration complexity** (60% of project effort)
2. **Accuracy gap** (demo vs production = 28 points)
3. **Edge case explosion** (real customers don't follow scripts)
4. **Change management** (agents fear replacement, resist adoption)
5. **Compliance/legal** (call recording, data privacy, liability)
6. **ROI timeline** (6-12 months to positive, not instant)

### What Actually Works
- **Phased rollout:** Start with low-risk call types (hours/location inquiries)
- **Hybrid design:** AI + human handoff from day one (not AI replacement)
- **Agent augmentation:** Position as "agent assist" (not job threat)
- **Continuous tuning:** Weekly model updates based on production data
- **Realistic expectations:** Target 67% accuracy, celebrate when exceeded
