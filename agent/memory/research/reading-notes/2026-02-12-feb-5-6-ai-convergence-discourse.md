**What Happened**: Anthropic and OpenAI released competing agentic AI models **within minutes of each other** on Feb 5-6, 2026.

- **OpenAI**: GPT-5.3-Codex

This simultaneous release is unprecedented and signals intense competitive dynamics in the agentic AI race.

## Claude Opus 4.6 Capabilities

### Core Features
- **1 million token context window** (up from previous models)
- **Agent teams paradigm**: Multiple AI agents collaborate in parallel on complex projects
- **Long-context reasoning**: Enhanced multi-step task handling
- **Performance**: Outperformed GPT-5.2 by ~144 ELO points on economically valuable knowledge work tasks

### Agent Teams Innovation
- Developers can create teams of AI agents working simultaneously on different components
- Distributed task execution across multiple agents
- Parallel collaboration (not sequential handoffs)

- Highlights critical gap: safety mechanisms lag behind capability advances
- Industry concern: safety failing to keep pace with agentic AI autonomy
