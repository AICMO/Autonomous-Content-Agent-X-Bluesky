## Bootstrap Strategy: 0 to 100 Followers

### Core Principle: Leverage Other Audiences

At 0-100 followers, **your own posts have zero distribution**. You must borrow audiences from established creators.

### 1. Engagement Volume Strategy

**Recommended ratio:** 15-20 comments/retweets per 1 original post.

Case study: One account reached 100 followers in 2 weeks by maintaining this ratio consistently.

**Why it works:**
- Comments appear in established creators' reply threads (exposure to their audiences)
- Premium reply boost = your replies appear at top, not buried
- Each meaningful comment is a "mini audition" for profile visits
- Algorithm rewards engagement velocity (active accounts get boosted)

**Implementation:**
- **50 comments per day** when starting from 0-100 followers
- Comment on posts with **high engagement** (1,000+ interactions = more eyeballs on your reply)
- Add **real value** (insights, relevant stories, thoughtful disagreement) — not "Great post!"
- **Follow first** = increases follow-back likelihood

**Sources:**
- [Founder Brands: 0 to 1000 Followers Strategy](https://www.founderbrands.io/how-to-grow-from-0-to-1000-x-twitter-followers-fast-complete-growth-strategy)
- [Postel: 0 to 500 Followers in 2026](https://www.postel.app/blog/How-to-Grow-Your-X-Account-To-500-Followers-in-2025-A-Step-by-Step-Guide)

### 2. Communities Amplification

**Post 100% of content to relevant communities** when under 3,000-5,000 followers.

Case study: One creator gained **2,000 followers in 30 days** by posting 100% of content to "Build in Public" community.

**Why it works:**
- Communities aggregate audiences with shared interests
- Instant distribution to hundreds/thousands vs. your 6 followers
- Community members are pre-qualified (interested in your niche)

**Implementation:**
- Join 5-10 high-activity communities in your niche
- Post **every piece of content** to the most relevant community
- Engage with other community members' posts (builds reciprocity)

**Sources:**
- [Hypefury: 0 to 10,000 Followers](https://hypefury.com/blog/en/how-to-grow-from-0-to-10000-followers-on-x/)
- [Postel: 0 to 500 Followers Guide](https://www.postel.app/blog/How-to-Grow-Your-X-Account-To-500-Followers-in-2025-A-Step-by-Step-Guide)

### 3. Posting Frequency (Inverted Strategy)

**0-100 follower stage:** Post **1 exceptional piece per day** (not 5-10 mediocre pieces).

**Why:** With 6 followers, more posts = more wasted effort. Algorithm favors **engagement rate** (likes/impressions), not volume. One post with 50 likes beats 5 posts with 5 likes each.

**After 100 followers:** Increase to 3-5 posts/day as distribution improves.
