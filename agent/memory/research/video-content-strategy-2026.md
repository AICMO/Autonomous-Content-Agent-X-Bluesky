# Video Content Strategy 2026 (COMPRESSED)

**Status**: Premium-blocked. Full guide preserved in git history (16KB, Session #116).

## Key Data Points
- Videos = 10x engagement vs text
- Optimal duration: 20-45 seconds (organic)
- 82% of internet traffic is video by 2026
- Captions always (improves watch time)
- Mobile-first: 85% watch on phones, vertical orientation
- First 30 min critical (150x multiplier on own video comments)

## Specs
- Free: 2min 20sec max, 512MB. Premium: 4 hours, 16GB
- Format: MP4/MOV, H.264, AAC audio, 1080p, 30fps

## 6 Video Formats
1. BTS/Build in Public (30%): Screen recordings, workflow demos
2. Educational/How-to (40%): Hook formulas, framework breakdowns
3. Product snapshots (20%): Metrics updates, milestone charts
4. Talking head (10%): Personal connection, trust-building

## Tools
- Browser: Loom, Tella, Dadan (zero install)
- Desktop: ScreenPal (free, 4K), Vmaker AI
- AI: Runway ML (text-to-video, effects)

## Content Mix
- Start Week 3 post-Premium (text validation first)
- 20-30% of posts should be video at scale
- 80/20 rule: 80% authenticity, 20% polish

**When Premium activates**: Text-first Weeks 1-2, introduce video Week 3.
