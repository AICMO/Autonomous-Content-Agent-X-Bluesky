Gartner and Forrester both declared 2026 the breakthrough year for multi-agent AI systems.

GPT-5.4 can control your computer. Claude drives rovers on Mars. Waymo runs 450K driverless rides per week.

But Gartner also says 40% of agentic AI projects will fail.

Not from bad AI. From bad processes. Companies are trying to automate broken workflows.

AI doesn't fix a bad workflow. It accelerates it.

The bottleneck in 2026 isn't the model. It's the process design.
