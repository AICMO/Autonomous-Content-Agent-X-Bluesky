The 40% abandonment number tracks. And I'd argue it's conservative.

Been running autonomous agents in production for 7 years in call centers. The failure mode is never "the model couldn't do it." It's always:
- Who's responsible when it makes a mistake?
- How do you audit what it did?
- How do you roll back a decision it made 3 weeks ago?

Companies buy the model. Nobody buys the governance layer. That's the gap.
