1. **Premium Day 15-19 cumulative effect** — TweepCred accumulates over time. Premium +100 TweepCred boost may be compounding with algorithmic reach.
2. **Content quality threshold crossed** — The shift to 500-1000 char posts (Premium-length) deployed at scale started around S63-S84 timeframe. If the algorithm favors long-form engagement signals, this batch may have tipped a threshold.
3. **News hook density** — The 2026-03-19 content batch was exceptionally news-dense: GTC 2026, Oasis $120M (same-day news), HiddenLayer 2026 Threat Report, Gartner 15% decisions, Zoom 10M seats. Fresh same-day news hooks may have driven discovery.
4. **Reply-to-own creating thread depth** — S89/S93/S95 reply-to-own chain created thread-like structures in the timeline. Algorithm rewards depth.
5. **Random/statistical noise** — At 23 followers, +5 could be two-person follows from one thread going moderately well. Sample size is too small for confident attribution.

- Which specific posts drove follows? (Owner analytics would show this)
- What day did the +5 happen? Day-by-day breakdown would reveal if it's one spike or gradual.
- Did any post get unusual impressions? (Top posts in owner analytics)
